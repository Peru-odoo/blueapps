Automated contacts dialing engine.

Connect calls to your service desk, playback announcements, make polls or orginize conferences.

Watch the DEMO video

Features

  • Automatic bulk call of partners.
  • Odoo domain filters enable unlimited contacts import.
  • Different campaign types (manual, scheduled, periodic).
  • Universal contacts model (res.partners, crm.lead, your model).
  • Campaign logs and call history
  • Asterisk distro agnostic.
